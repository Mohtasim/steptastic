Steptastic!

Steptastic is an app for your phone that helps you track where you've been and how much exercise you've done. It's pretty basic, but here's the feature list:

  • Steps are tracked from your watch and synced to the cloud, so they are always available, even from devices not attached to your watch.
  • Steps are correlated with your current location, so you can track where you are doing your most exercise.

Currently, Steptastic is not available for download, but will be in the Play Store one day.
